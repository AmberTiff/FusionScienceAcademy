Admission Process

How to Apply at Fusion Science Academy

Step 1: Online Application

Begin the application process by filling out our online application form. You will need to provide personal details and basic academic information.

Start Your Application

Step 2: Submit Required Documents

Once you've completed the application form, submit the following required documents:

  • Previous school transcripts
  • Letter of recommendation from a teacher
  • Student's birth certificate
  • Completed medical forms

Step 3: Entrance Exam

After reviewing your application, you will be invited to take our entrance exam. This will assess your academic readiness and help us tailor your educational experience.

Step 4: Interview

Successful candidates will be scheduled for an interview with our admissions team. This is an opportunity for us to learn more about you, your interests, and your academic goals.

Step 5: Admission Decision

Following the interview and exam, you will receive your admission decision via email. If accepted, you will receive further details on how to proceed with enrollment.
